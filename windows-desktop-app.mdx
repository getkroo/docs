The Kroo desktop app gives your team a native Windows experience for accessing construction project data, with automatic updates built in.

## Prerequisites

- **Windows 10 or later** (64-bit)
- An active Kroo account — contact [implementations@getkroo.com](mailto:implementations@getkroo.com) if you need access

## Download

Download the Kroo Windows installer:

<Card title="Download Kroo for Windows" icon="download" href="https://dkq5g4kc673o0.cloudfront.net/downloads/windows-app/kroo.Setup.1.0.0.exe">
  Windows installer (.exe) — v1.0.0
</Card>

## Installation

<Steps>
<Step title="Run the installer">
  Double-click the downloaded `kroo.Setup.1.0.0.exe` file to begin installation.

  <Info>
  Windows may show a SmartScreen prompt since the app is new. Click **More info** then **Run anyway** to proceed.
  </Info>
</Step>

<Step title="Sign in">
  The app will launch automatically after installation. Sign in with your Kroo credentials.
</Step>

<Step title="Verify the app is working">
  After signing in, confirm you can see your project data.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Auto-Updates

The app checks for updates automatically on launch. Updates download in the background and install when you close the app. You never need to manually reinstall.
